A simple mod that adds ducks to Minecraft.

### Ducks

- Ducks are neutral mobs (they will attack when provoked).
- They can be bred by 'Bread Crumbs' (a new item crafted by putting bread in the crafting grid).
- Ducks will stop attacking the player if they are holding bread crumbs in their hand.
- Ducks can be found near river biomes (on lanad or swimming).
- They drop feathers and kelp when killed.
